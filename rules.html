Realm Rules

Please follow these rules to ensure a fair and fun environment for all players.

General & PvP Rules

  • No Invis/Small skins
  • No hacks of any sort
  • No combat logging
  • No Day/Offline Raiding
  • Stay inside the map boundaries
  • No abusing glitches or exploits
  • No asking staff for items or special treatment
  • No under armor killing
  • No using pvp clients

Building & Item Rules

  • Put a sign on your base with your team/player name
  • No boats
  • No minecarts
  • No leaving auto gens on when you log off

Consequences

Breaking any rule will result in a warning. 3 warnings will lead to Prison. Prolonged or severe rule-breaking will result in a permanent ban.

Report a Player

See someone breaking the rules? Please report them to a staff member with evidence (screenshots or video) to help us keep the Realm enjoyable for everyone!
